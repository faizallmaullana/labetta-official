Here, we present to you a very special poetry book called “Life Upon A Poem” by Cintya Amanda Labetta.

Life Upon A Poem is not just a mere writing,
but a poem with something contained within its meaning,
it told us about story of life; full of complexity;
talking both about sorrow and happiness,
full of its surprising moment,
from moment of silence till moment of truth,
its the interpretation of every moment be it the one thats long gone but worth to keep,
or the one that still exists. This book is divided into 4 big parts,
1. Special journey of life;
2. Love;
3. Natures and Places; and
4. Humanity Therefore,
